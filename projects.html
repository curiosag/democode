Financial Provider Data Processing

A series of individual projects spread out over some years. Each covered provider data delivery logic, provider data format parsing, evaluation, cleansing and storage to a repository, covering provider data mutation rules, extraction and update of descriptive data of provider meta-data and codes. Analysis involved identification and verification of sources for meta-data and descriptive data, in-depth resolution of gaps and ambiguities, and eventually model mapping from the provider model to the internally used meta-data. Providers covered were Bloomberg Backoffice and Per Security, Reuters RMDS, RDB, Equities and Fixed Income and Swift Iso15022. In a consulting role I dealt with Wertpapiermanagement, Ftid, Reuters Select and general XML-feed processing.

technology: Delphi

roles: consulting, analysis, design, TPM, integration, test, 3rd level support, maintenance
